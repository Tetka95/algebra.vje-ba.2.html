Our mission is to empower young Students to be the inventors and creators.

In a world where so much is being done for technology and so little for the environment, education is not even a part of most of discussions.

We at Studytonight believe that by widening the reach of education, by making it freely available, so much can be achieved.

And this yourney started in 2013 when a young boy thought "wouldn't it be great to have a website, with simple tutorials for programming languages, just like a friend would teach you!" , and Studytonight waas born.
